== Description ==

The #1 Content Subscription Platform. A powerful WordPress membership plugin that integrates your site with Pelcro's Content Subscription Platform. [Pelcro](https://www.pelcro.com) is a comprehensive online platform that provides you with all the tools and insight necessary to drive subscription revenue from your audience. Using secure payment gateways like [Stripe](https://stripe.com) & [Paypal](https://www.paypal.com). 


**Pelcro is the #1 Content Subscription Platform on Google because it is:**

- **Flexible :** Choose a [paywall](http://blog.pelcro.com/what-is-a-paywall/) that works for you. Create a **metered paywall** for your blog to give your audience a number of free articles first. Turn individual articles into **premium content** for your members. It can all be done straight from your dashboard.

- **Comprehensive :** It's not just about the paywalls. You have a **CRM (Customer Relationship Manager)** with individual membership records, a **Support Dashboard** for refunds, customer support, billing and invoice management, and a full **Analytics Dashboard**.

- **Data Driven :** The ability to offer coupon codes, target specific countries and devices as well as all of your A/B tests will appear directly on your analytics dashboard. Gain a full understanding of your users and how well the campaigns are doing based on conversions that our machine learning algorithms will help you optimize.


Setup takes minutes so you can start generating recurring revenue from your audience in no time. [Sign up](https://www.pelcro.com/auth/register?utm_source=wp-plugin-directory) now and try it for yourself.


Here's a QuickStart guide to help you get started: [http://docs.pelcro.com/quickstart](http://docs.pelcro.com/quickstart)

Our API allows you to do even more, Checkout our [documentation](https://pelcro.docs.apiary.io).

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Install the plugin through the WordPress.
2. Activate the pelcro plugin from your "plugins" dashboard.
3. Select "Pelcro" in your settings section on WordPress.
4. Click on "Get your site ID" or visit https://www.pelcro.com to register your site and get started.
5. Access the Administrator menu (top right corner of your Pelcro dashboard) and select the Integration page.
6. Copy the site ID and paste it in the site ID field in your Pelcro setting page in WordPress.

Please email support@pelcro.com for questions.
